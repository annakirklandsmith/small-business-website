About Us
Who We Serve

On Location NC customizes crew and equipment rental packages for film, multimedia, video, or still photography. We serve media professionals and businesses. We are open by appointment only for pickup and returns by staff and approved contractors.

Even More Equipment

If you don’t see it on our list, please ask. We are continually expanding our inventory and services. We also outsource gear often through our relationships with many of our local contractors that own equipment and affiliated vendors along the East Coast. Basically, if you give us enough time and the item is available we can get it.

Seemless Experience

We want to give you a seamless work experience while working in our area. Your crew and equipment can be placed on one invoice. Equipment delivery and pickup available. Our crews and equipment are fully insured with workers’ compensation and general liability insurance. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover cards.
